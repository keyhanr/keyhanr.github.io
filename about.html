ABOUT

Past

I was born at the ripe age of 0, just in time to grow up in the digital age. My first interests were toy cars and ice cream, but soon I had the luxury of access to the World Wide Web. There I would spend time on Neopets or delving deep into the pits of Wikipedia. Outside of the computer I found an interest in collecting coins and stamps. In elementary school I joined the flag football team, and in high school I joined the yearbook commitee and enlisted as a photographer for The Spyglass, the school newspaper.

Present

I am now in my third year at University of Toronto, specializing in computer science with a focus in AI.
I enjoy movies that can make me really feel, and music that makes me realize we're on a rock falling through space. I enjoy casual sports with my friends, from half-court basketball to just tossing a football around. I'm lucky enough to live somewhere that I can enjoy nature and still be close to the city. I do some photography since I feel it would be a waste to not capture pretty moments.

Future

Realistically, I hope to work on projects that will improve the world. I think we have a long way to go before we can consider even our current systems optimized. With the computational power availible to us I think it's a waste if we don't use it fully to streamline every aspect of life.
My deepest dream is to develop an AI that can do just that, analyze the way human infrastructure is designed and give suggestions to improve it.
