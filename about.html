ABOUT

Past

I also found an interest in collecting coins, stamps, and old books. Present

I'm now graduating from University of Toronto with a degree incomputer science and cognitive science.
I most enjoy movies and music that make me really feel. I enjoy casual sports with my friends, from half-court basketball to just tossing a football around. I'm lucky enough to live somewhere that I can enjoy nature and still be close to the city. I do some photography since I like to preserve feelings.

Future

My deepest dream is to develop an AI that can do just that, analyze the way human infrastructure is designed and give suggestions to improve it.
